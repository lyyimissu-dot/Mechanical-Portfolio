## ## 2. System Overview

A typical stacker crane system includes:

### **Core Mechanical Structure**
- **Double-mast structure** (up to 35 m, sometimes assembled in 2â€“3 mast sections)
- **Top guiding rail** and **bottom track** (straight or curved)
- **Traveling chassis**
- **Hoisting platform / lifting carriage**
- **Fork system** (single-deep, double-deep, or adjustable mechanisms)
- **Control cabinet & operator platform**

### **Main Functions**
- Horizontal aisle travel  
- Vertical hoisting  
- Automated pallet/container handling  
- Accurate load positioning with sensor systems
