## ðŸ“Œ 4. Typical Design Process

The following diagram illustrates my general design workflow:

1. **Requirement Analysis**  
   Load, speed, stroke, pallet specification, warehouse layout

2. **Concept Development**  
   Mechanism selection (chain lifting, fork type, mast configuration)

3. **Parametric 3D Modeling**  
   SolidEdge ordered + synchronous modeling  
   Modular design based on key dimensions

4. **Structural Evaluation**  
   Stress calculation, deflection targets, simplified FEA

5. **Detailing & Manufacturing Preparation**  
   2D drawings, welding symbols, GD&T, BOM

6. **Review & Optimization**  
   Manufacturability review  
   Assembly feasibility  
   Safety and maintenance considerations

7. **Onsite Support & Final Verification**  
   Alignment check  
   Troubleshooting  
   Update design standards based on feedback
