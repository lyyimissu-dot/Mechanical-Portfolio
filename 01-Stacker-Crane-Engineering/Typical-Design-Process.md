# ## 1. Requirement Clarification

### **1.1 Customer Requirement Collection**
- Warehouse size (aisle width, aisle height, aisle length)
- Pallet / container size & weight
- Operating temperature & humidity  
- Fire-proof / explosion-proof requirements  
- Corrosion resistance / stainless steel requirement  
- Speed requirements (hoisting speed, travel speed, fork speed)
- Required loading capacity (e.g., 1.1t / 2t / 3t / custom loads)

### **1.2 On-site Investigation (if needed)**
- Foundation condition & levelness  
- Beam or roof interference  
- Reserved electrical interfaces  
- Track mounting positions  
- Existing equipment constraints  

### **1.3 External Component Specification**
- Motor/drive brand preferences  
- Sensor brand preferences  
- Power supply system type  
- Conductor rail layout  
- Safety standard requirements  

Outcome â†’ **Design Input Specification Document**

# ## 2. Concept Design

### **2.1 Mechanical Configuration Proposal**
Based on the requirements, propose options such as:
- Single-mast vs. double-mast  
- Fork type:
  - Single-deep  
  - Double-deep  
  - Adjustable variable-spacing  
  - Dedicated forks (for barrels, rolls, tanks)

### **2.2 Preliminary Calculations**
- Approximate mast height  
- Hoisting stroke
- Calculation of the length of the lifting drag chain
- Required motor torque & power  
- Overall structural size estimation  
- Strength considerations for special loads  

Outcome â†’ **Concept Drawings & Initial Approval**

- Fork system

### **3.2 Component Design & Modeling**
- Wheels / pulleys  
- Shaft connections  
- Welded structures  
- Safety stops & bumpers  
- Mounting plates, brackets  

### **3.3 Motion & Interference Check**
- Horizontal travel clearance  
- Vertical movement clearance  
- Fork extension interference simulation  
- Curved-track motion simulation (if applicable)
